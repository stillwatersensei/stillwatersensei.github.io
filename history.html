History of Tai Chi and Qigong

Tai Chi and Qigong are Chinese movement traditions that combine posture, breath, attention, and gentle flow. Qigong is a broad family of practices for cultivating breath and energy awareness. Tai Chi developed as an internal martial art and later became widely practiced for balance, coordination, relaxation, and health-focused movement.

Stillwater adapts the spirit of these traditions into a simple seated practice designed for accessibility, calm, and daily consistency.
