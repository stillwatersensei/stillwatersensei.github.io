History of Tai Chi and Qigong

Qigong is an ancient Chinese practice combining breath, posture, gentle movement, and focused awareness. It developed through health, meditation, and martial traditions.

Tai Chi, or Taijiquan, developed later as an internal martial art. Today it is widely practiced for balance, relaxation, mobility, and mindful movement.

Stillwater adapts these ideas into a gentle seated format for accessible daily practice.
